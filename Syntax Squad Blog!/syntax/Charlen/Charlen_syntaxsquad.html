My Favorite Music

“As You Find Me” by Hillsong UNITED is my favorite song because it speaks straight to the heart. Every time I listen to it, I'm reminded that God's love doesn't wait for me to be perfect—He meets me right where I am. Even in my struggles, doubts, and weaknesses, His grace remains constant. This song inspires me to keep going, to trust, and to believe that I am enough in His eyes. It's more than worship —it's a reminder that I am deeply loved, accepted, and valued no matter what. Every lyric feels like God whispering, “You are mine, just as I find you.”

My Blog

Every week, I take a journey that's more than just a commute—it's an adventure. From the busy streets where I ride a jeep, to the calm waves I cross by boat, and the final stretch on a motorbike leading me home to Talim Island—each trip feels like stepping into a new chapter of my story. This blog is where I share those rides—the challenges, the quiet moments, the beauty of travel between two worlds: the fast pace of student life in STI and the serene life back home on the island. If you're curious about unique travel routes, island living, or simply finding meaning in the paths we take, then welcome aboard my weekly journey.
