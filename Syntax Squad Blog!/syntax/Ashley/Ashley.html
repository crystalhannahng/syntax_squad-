My Favorite Music

My chosen favorite song is ‘I Like Me Better’ by Lauv because I really enjoy its chill and relaxing vibe. It’s one of those songs that I can listen to anytime, whether I’m studying, commuting, or just relaxing. It always puts me in a good mood, which is why I chose it as my favorite.
